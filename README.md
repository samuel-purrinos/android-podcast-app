# Podcast Explorer - Discover and Listen to Popular Podcasts on your Android Device.

Listen to the Top 100 Podcasts on iTunes

Do you love podcasts? Do you want to discover new and exciting shows from around the world? Do you want to listen to them on your Android device with ease and convenience?

If you answered yes to any of these questions, then this app is for you!

This app lets you listen to the top 100 podcasts on iTunes, updated daily. You can browse by country, genre, or popularity. You can also search for your favorite podcasts by name or keyword.

You can stream or download episodes for offline listening. You can also subscribe to your favorite podcasts and get notified when new episodes are available.

With this app, you will never run out of podcasts to listen to. Whether you are into comedy, news, sports, music, true crime, or anything else, you will find something that suits your taste and mood.

Download this app today and enjoy the best podcasts on iTunes!

## Installation

    1. Clone or download the app from the GitHub repository.
    2. Open Android Studio and select "Open an existing Android Studio project" from the welcome screen.
    3. Navigate to the directory where you cloned or downloaded the app, and select the project folder.
    4. Wait for Android Studio to finish building the project.
    5. Connect your Android device to your computer using a USB cable, and make sure that USB debugging is enabled on your device.
    6. In Android Studio, click on the "Run" button in the toolbar or select "Run > Run 'app'" from the menu.
    7. Select your connected device from the list of available devices, and click "OK".
    8. Wait for Android Studio to install the app on your device and launch it.

## Usage

Main Screen:

    Shows a list of the 100 most popular podcasts according to Apple.

    You can search for podcasts by typing in a text string that will be used to search for podcast titles and author names.
    The search results should update immediately as you type.
    When you tap on a podcast, you will be taken to a screen with more details about it.

Podcast Detail Screen:

    Shows the image of the podcast, its title, author, and description.
    Has a section that displays the number of episodes and a list of them, including their title, release date, and duration.
    When you tap on the title of an episode, you will be taken to a screen with more details about it.

Episode Detail Screen:

    The image, title, and author of the podcast should all be links to the podcast detail screen.
    The screen displays the title of the podcast, its description, and a basic audio player to play the podcast.
